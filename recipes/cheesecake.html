Cheesecake

This cheesecake is a simple recipe but a delicious tasting one. It has few ingredients and it could be garnished with whatever you like (frosting, jam, chocolate). Here, we will be making a plain cheesecake. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese, 2 sticks (8 oz ea)
  • Condensed sweet milk, 4 oz can
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 eggs
  • Vanilla extract, 1/2 tablespoon
  • Pie crust, 9 in

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit
  2. In a blender place all of the ingredients and blend well
  3. Pour blended mix into the crust
  4. Bake for about 70 minutes
  5. Cheesecake should look inflated when taken out, let it cool until it settles and garnish if desired.
