Vampiro

A vampiro is a sweet drink with tequila and spiced vodka the sangrita mix along with the other ingredients will mask the taste of the liquor so beware as this is a sneak of a drink.

Ingredients

  • Tequila
  • Squirt
  • Pineapple juice
  • Spicy Tamarindo Smirnoff
  • Lime juice
  • Tajin
  • Vodka
  • Viuda de Sanchez sangrita

Steps:

  1. Fill a pint glass with ice. Salt the rim, if desired
  2. Add the juice of 1 (one) lime and some tajin as desired
  3. Add half an ounce of tequila
  4. Add half an ounce of vodka
  5. Add half an ounce of Spicy Tamarindo Smirnoff
  6. Add two or three ounces of Viuda de Sanchez
  7. Add about three ounces of pineapple juice
  8. Fill cup with Squirt and stirr
